# Export AI Chat History: Complete Guide to Save Your AI Conversations

**Save your ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and AI chat history in minutes.** Whether you're switching platforms, backing up important conversations, or building a personal AI knowledge base, exporting your AI chat history is essential.

- **Backup Important Conversations** - Don't lose valuable insights, code snippets, or creative work
- **Switch Between AI Platforms** - Move from ChatGPT to Claude, Gemini, or other AI assistants
- **Build a Knowledge Base** - Create a searchable archive of your AI interactions
- **Share Collaboration** - Export specific chats to share with team members
- **Compliance & Records** - Maintain records for professional or legal purposes

## How to Export AI Chat History (All Major Platforms)

### Export ChatGPT Chat History

1. **Go to ChatGPT Settings**
   - Click your profile icon (bottom-left)
   - Select **Settings** → **Data Controls**

2. **Request Export**
   - Click **Export Data**
   - Confirm your request
   - You'll receive an email with a download link (usually within hours)

3. **Download Your Data**
   - Click the link in your email
   - Download the `.zip` file containing your conversations in JSON format

**What You Get:** Complete chat history in `conversations.json` format, including all messages, timestamps, and model information.

### Export Claude Chat History (Anthropic)

1. **Access Claude Settings**
   - Click your profile icon
   - Navigate to **Settings** → **Account**

2. **Export Your Data**
   - Select **Export my data**
   - Confirm the request
   - Receive email with download link

**Format:** JSON file with all conversation threads

### Export Google Gemini (Bard) Chat History

1. **Google Takeout Method**
   - Go to [takeout.google.com](https://takeout.google.com)
   - Deselect all, then select **Bard** or **Gemini**
   - Click **Next** then **Create Export**

2. **Direct Export** (if available)
   - In Gemini, click Settings
   - Select **Export conversations**

**Format:** JSON or HTML depending on method

### Export Microsoft Copilot Chat History

1. **Account Settings**
   - Navigate to account.microsoft.com
   - Go to **Privacy** → **Download your data**

2. **Select Copilot Data**
   - Choose Copilot conversations
   - Request download
